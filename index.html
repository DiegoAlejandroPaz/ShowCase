About Me

Hello, my name is Diego Paz, I am a highly devoted individual whose drive for creativity pushes me into exceeding expectations every time. I am a passionate artist and designer based in Nashville, TN. I've had a deep love for art since childhood, I have spent countless hours honing my skills and exploring various mediums and techniques.

My artistic journey has been a constant exploration of self-expression, emotion, and storytelling through visual art. From vibrant portraits that capture the essence of my subjects to abstract creations that evoke a sense of wonder, my work aims to provoke thought and engage the viewer on a deeper level.
